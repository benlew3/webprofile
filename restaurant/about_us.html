Who We Are

Hello, we are a bunch of random chinese guys who love ramen. We love it soooo much we decided to open our own place here in marin! My partner and I went to Japan to learn how to cook traditional japanese ramen, in order to bring back the proper taste of ramen.

We live, cook, and BREATH ramen. It has taken us many hours slaving away in the kitchens to find our flavor. We hope you enjoy our passion. The ramen we have picked to serve have a variety of flavors and consistancy.
